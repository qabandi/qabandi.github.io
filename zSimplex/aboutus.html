ABOUT US

We Have The Pleasure to Introduce Ourselves as: “Al-Hadi Glass Industries”. Al-Hadi Glass Industries is a New Leading Glass Processor Factory in Kuwait with The Latest and Most Advanced Machines in Kuwait from The World’s Leading Manufacturers (Finland:Glaston, Austria:LiSEC, Italy:Bavelloni) with Capability 300,000 m² of Glass per year / one shift. The Glass Industry Has Changed to Be a Highly Preforming Product that Guarantees Protection, Thermal & Acoustic Insulation, Solar Control and Energy Conservation. Our Competitive Edge Lies in Our Capability to Process the High Performance Glass Types “Triple Silver, Low-E, etc.” According to the Requirements of EN Standards; and Our Quality Team Practices the Most Up-To-Date Quality Control Tests and Procedures, Guaranties Customers an Impeccable Product.
