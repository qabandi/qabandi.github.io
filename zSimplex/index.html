ABOUT US

Al-Hadi industries was first established 1973 by the late Mohammed Hadi Al-Awadi. The operations are now located in the subhan industrial area in Kuwait and in response to the increased demand for processed glasses, such as tempered, insulated double glazing units and laminated glass, Al-Hadi Glass Industries has established a new factory. The facility encompasses an impressive 10,000m2 in area and equipped with the very latest technological glass processing machines from the world’s leading manufacturers, such as Glaston of Finland and Lisec of Austria to provide the best possible quality glass products anywhere in the world. As an added service Al-Hadi has also formed joint venture with leading companies to assist in the field of installation of monumental curtain willing for high rise buildings. Al-Hadi are deeply committed to meeting the needs of our customers and we will constantly focus on customer satisfaction and concentrate on reliable performance for world best quality standards for every process. Glass today is used to perform many functions other than its primary role of allowing light to enter a building. Its applications can be visual, mechanical, structural, decorative, thermal, architectural or artistic and even a combination of any or all of these aspects. Al-Hadi Glass will provide a range of services, including design assistance, specification guidelines, product development, testing and assessment to cover all these elements and thus achieve excellence and unique standards in the field of glass processing.
